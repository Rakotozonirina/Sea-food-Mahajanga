Where to Enjoy Seafood in Mahajanga

Beachside Restaurants

Many restaurants along the coastline offer stunning views of the ocean along with their seafood specialties. Dining here as the sun sets over the water is an unforgettable experience.

Local Markets

The bustling markets of Mahajanga are a great place to sample fresh seafood. Vendors often grill fish and shellfish right on the spot, allowing you to enjoy a quick and tasty meal.

Fishing Villages

For a more authentic experience, visit the nearby fishing villages where you can watch fishermen bring in their daily catch and enjoy seafood prepared in traditional Malagasy style.
