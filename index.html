Sustainability and Conservation

As the demand for seafood grows, so does the importance of sustainable fishing practices. Many locals in Mahajanga are committed to preserving their marine resources, ensuring that future generations can continue to enjoy the bounty of the sea.

When dining in Mahajanga, consider supporting restaurants and vendors that prioritize sustainability.
