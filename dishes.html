Must-Try Seafood Dishes in Mahajanga

Grilled Lobster (Omasa)

Lobster is a star of Mahajanga's seafood scene. Often grilled over an open flame and seasoned with local spices, garlic and a squeze of lime, this dish is a must-try for anyyone visiting the city.

Served with a side of Coconut rice or fresh salad, it's a true taste of the ocean.

Coconut Crab Curry (Crab au Cocotier)

Coconut crab, a local delicacy, is often cooked in a rich, creamy cocnut curry infused with ginger, garlic, and chili.

The sweet and savory flavors of this dish perfectly complement the tender crab meat, making it a favorite among locals and tourists.

Octopus Salad (Salad de Poulpe)

Fresh octopus is boiled until tender, then tossed with tomatoes, onions, green peppers, and a zesty vinaigrette.

This light and refreshing dish is perfect for a hot day by the beach.

Fried Shrimp with Garlic Butter (Crevettes à l'Ail)

Juicy shrimp are sautéed in garlic butter and served with a side of cripsy fries or steamed vegetables.

Simple yet incredibly flavorful, this dish highlights the natural sweetness of the shrimp.

Fish Kebabs (Brochettes de Poisson)

Freshly caught fish is marinated in a blend of spices skewered, and grilled to perfection.

Often served with a spicy chili sauce or a tangy tamarind glaze, these kebabs are a popular street food option.
